Social Media Do's and Don'ts
Do’s:
•	Keep LinkedIn profile professional. 
•	Fact-check before sharing information. 
•	Use privacy settings wisely. 
•	Separate personal and academic accounts. 
•	Think carefully before commenting. 
Don’ts:
•	Post personal conflicts online. 
•	Share confidential college info. 
•	Accept friend requests from strangers. 
•	Overshare daily routines. 
•	Use offensive language or memes.
