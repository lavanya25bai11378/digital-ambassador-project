PREVENTION CHECKLIST
Title: Stay Safe Online – College Students

1. Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone.  
2. Verify the source of any payment request before responding.  
3. Enable UPI app’s PIN and biometric authentication.  
4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online transactions.  
5. Regularly monitor bank statements for suspicious activity.  
6. Keep devices updated with latest security patches.  
7. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.  
8. Report suspicious activity immediately via:
   - National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
   - Helpline: 1930
