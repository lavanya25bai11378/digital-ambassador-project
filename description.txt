I created this infographic using Canva to spread awareness about digital literacy among students. It covers key areas such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. I used simple icons, short points, and a clear layout so that the information is easy to understand at a glance. 

While designing, I found it interesting to experiment with colors and visual elements to make the infographic attractive. However, the main challenge was fitting all important information into a single page without making it look cluttered. This task helped me understand how visuals can effectively communicate important ideas.
